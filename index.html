Methodology

A structured consulting framework

Every project follows a deliberate process — from understanding the business context to validating the final solution. This approach ensures that design decisions are always grounded in evidence, not assumption.

01
Understand Context
Engage stakeholders, define scope, and map the organizational environment to establish a clear project brief and success criteria.
02
Identify Problems
Apply root cause analysis, user interviews, and data review to distinguish symptoms from underlying business challenges.
03
Analyze Requirements
Document functional and non-functional requirements through structured elicitation, producing clear specifications for all stakeholders.
04
Model Processes
Visualize workflows, system interactions, and data flows using UML, BPMN, and entity-relationship models.
05
Design Solutions
Translate requirements into wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications — iterating on feedback from users and stakeholders.
06
Validate & Test
Develop comprehensive test scenarios, conduct user acceptance testing, and document findings against defined acceptance criteria.
07
Deliver Outcomes
Package deliverables with clear documentation, handoff materials, and strategic recommendations for implementation and next phases.
